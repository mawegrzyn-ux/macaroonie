Our Story

Ten years of cooking
for our neighbours.

years in Ware

One Thai opened on a quiet stretch of West Street in 2016, with a short menu, a few tables, and the kind of nervous optimism you only have when you're cooking your grandmother's recipes for strangers.

A decade later, those strangers turned into regulars, and the regulars turned into friends. The menu has grown — a little classic, a little modern, always honest — but the room is still small, the kitchen is still ours, and we still get a bit excited when a new face walks in.

We do things a touch differently here: lunch and dinner have their own rhythms, the curries are made in the morning, and we cook honestly — no MSG, no shortcuts, just real ingredients and the techniques our chefs have spent years getting right.
